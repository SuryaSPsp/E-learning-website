Angular JS Basics



Instructors: Alan, Samvel Course Starts:19-12-2020
Duration: 19 hours Course Ends:15-06-2021

AngularJS is a JavaScript-based open-source front-end web application framework. It was developed by Google and is now maintained by a community of developers.

AngularJS is designed to simplify the development and testing of dynamic single-page applications (SPAs) by providing a framework for client-side MVC (Model-View-Controller) and MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) architectures.
